Instructions for providing your comments

  1. Open the ADA Connect site and go to the WG 11.6 space by clicking on this link. You might need to log into ADA Connect. Use your regular ADA Connect credentials to log in.
  2. Search for whatever you are commenting about: if somebody already left a similar comment to what you were thinking about, add to the existing discussion.
  3. If you don't find a discussion that is relevant to what you want to comment, you need to create a new Discussion on the ADA Connect site, in the WG11.6 space. Pick a subject for your comment: Please start the subject line with "ADA-1107: ". That will make it easier for anyone to understand what this discussion is about, since all discussions go in the same folder.

If you have any problems, send an email to Toni Magni afm@case.edu.

Thank you!
